About the Illam

Joe Andrea Illam was started on 27 August 1998 with 15 children.

Today, the organization supports approximately 85 children by providing free accommodation, food, education, healthcare, clothing, and personal development opportunities.

Our goal is to help needy children grow into educated, responsible, and successful individuals while providing a safe and nurturing environment.

Our Purpose

🎯 Our Mission

To rescue, shelter, and empower vulnerable children by breaking the cycle of poverty through holistic care, stable emotional tracking, and robust educational structures.

👁️ Our Vision

A society where every child, regardless of their background, has access to basic human rights, equal educational paths, and the secure confidence to stand on their own feet.
