About Us

PHOTO PLACEHOLDER

Joe Andrea Illam was started on 27 August 1998 with 15 children.

Today, the organization supports approximately 85 children by providing free accommodation, food, education, healthcare, clothing and personal development opportunities.

Our goal is to help needy children grow into educated, responsible and successful individuals while providing a safe and nurturing environment.

Mission & Vision

Mission

To provide free food, education and shelter for needy children.

Vision

To support underprivileged children by providing a safe environment and opportunities for a better future.
