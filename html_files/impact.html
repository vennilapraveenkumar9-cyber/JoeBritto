Activities & Educational Exposure

To ensure holistic child development, we prioritize real-world experiences. We regularly organize weekend educational and recreational trips to expand their worldview and give them an active, joyous childhood.

Destinations include:

  • Parks, Movie Theatres & Temples / Churches
  • Thirumalai Nayakkar Mahal & Kalaignar Centenary Library
  • Chocolate Factories, Kutladampatti Falls & Zoo
  • Old-Age Homes (where students participate in celebrations & volunteer service during festivals like Diwali)

Exceptional Teacher Dedication

The teachers at Joe Andrea Illam demonstrate profound personal commitment to our cause. Despite drawing modest monthly salaries of approximately ₹6,000, they stay driven out of pure care for the children's futures.

"Many teachers choose to celebrate their own family functions and special occasions right here with the children, embracing them completely as part of their own families."
