Facilities & Services

We are dedicated to providing a safe, nurturing, and holistic ecosystem where underprivileged children can heal, learn, and grow into successful individuals. All of our services are provided completely free of charge.

🏠
Accommodation

A safe, clean, and secure living environment to call home.

📚
Education Support

Covering school expenses, textbooks, notebooks, and guidance.

🍎
Free Food & Nutrition

Three daily healthy, well-balanced, and nutritious meals.

🩺
Healthcare Support

Regular medical checks, emergency care, and health management.

👕
Clothing Assistance

Providing seasonal clothing, daily wear, and school uniforms.

⚽
Sports & Activities

Recreational trips, physical games, and talent development.
